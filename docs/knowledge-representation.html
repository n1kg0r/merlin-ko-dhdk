RDF production

The statements visualised in the conceptual model and the data listed in the Data Description are then translated into a RDF Turtle serialisation. To do so we used the URI presented in the previous step, while the properties are translated according to the chosen ontologies. Moreover, in order to provide a major interoperability of our data with external one, the entities having an authority are associated with it through the OWL property owl:sameAs. The authorities are provided by Wikidata DBpedia and VIAF for people, institutions and fictional characters and Geonames for places.

The serialisation has been verified thanks to IDL Lab Turtle validator.

RDF visualization

After the serialization, the data expressed in Turtle serialisation can be finally visualised as a knowledge graphl. The network of entities and properties is automatically drawn by RDF Grapher.
